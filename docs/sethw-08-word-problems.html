    \(120\) is what percent of \(520\text{?.}\)
    Your answers is : percent.
    Answer.
    \(\frac{120\cdot 100}{520}\)
    Solution.
    Solution:
    \begin{equation*} \frac{120}{520} \cdot 100 \% \approx 23.077 \% \end{equation*}

    3. local/PREP_2011_College_Algebra/07_Word_Problems_Interest_Mixture_Motion_Rates/lh1-3_39.pg.

    \(290\) is \(35\) percent of what number?
    Your answers is :
    Answer.
    \(\frac{290\cdot 100}{35}\)
    Solution.
    Solution:
    We let \(x\) be the unknown number. The problem states that \(35\) percent of \(x\) must be equal \(290\text{:}\)
    \begin{equation*} x \cdot \frac{35}{100} = 290 \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} x = 290 \cdot \frac{100}{35} = 828.571428571429 \end{equation*}
